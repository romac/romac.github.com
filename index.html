About me

My name is Romain Ruetschi, also known as Romac.

I am a Computer Science student at EPFL in Lausanne, Switzerland, where I have co-founded an association named Hackers at EPFL.

My main interests are related to functional programming, type theory, compilers and formal verification of software. I like exploring these topics in Haskell, Scala, and Rust.
