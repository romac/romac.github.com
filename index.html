About me

My name is Romain Ruetschi, also known as Romac.

I am a Computer Science student at EPFL in Lausanne, Switzerland,
where I've co-founded an association named Hackers at EPFL.

My main interests are related to functional programming, type theory, concurrency and compiler construction. I also care deeply about the Web, its surrounding technologies, and our right to privacy.

I'm into way too many thing these days, amongst which:

  • Haskell
  • Scala
  • JavaScript / React
  • Functional Reactive Programming, in Scala and JavaScript.
I also like to occasionally dabble with other programming languages, such as Idris, Erlang, C, and x86 assembly.
