About me

My name is Romain Ruetschi, also known as Romac.

I am a Computer Science student at EPFL in Lausanne, Switzerland,
where I've co-founded an association named Hackers at EPFL.

I am interested in functional programming, type theory, concurrency, and compiler construction. I also care deeply about the Web, its surrounding technologies, and our right to privacy.

These days I am mostly into:

  • Scala / Play
  • JavaScript / React
  • Reactive Extensions (Rx), both in Scala and JavaScript
  • ClojureScript / Om / core.async
But I also like to occasionally dabble with other programming languages, such as Haskell, Go, C, and x86 assembly.
